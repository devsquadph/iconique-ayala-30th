Frequently Asked Questions

How soon can I expect to see results?

You may start noticing improvements in your skin after just one session, with more visible results developing over the next few weeks as your skin naturally regenerates. For optimal results, multiple sessions may be recommended depending on your skin concerns.

How long is the facial treatment?

The Pico Laser facial treatment takes approximately 60 minutes.

How should I prepare for the treatment?

To ensure the best results, we recommend the following before your Pico Laser treatment:

  • Avoid sun exposure for at least a week prior to your session
  • Discontinue use of retinol, acids, or harsh exfoliants at least 3-5 days before treatment
  • Stay hydrated and keep your skin moisturized
  • Arrive with a clean face (free of makep and skincare products)

Our skin specialists will guide you through the process to ensure a safe and effective treatment.
